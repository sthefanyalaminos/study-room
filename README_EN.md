# ⏱️ Pomodoro Timer
A study environment built around the Pomodoro Technique. The interface adapts its color theme based on the time of day: Night, morning, or afternoon - and brings everything you need for a study session into one place: a timer, a to-do list, and a notes section.
 
---
 
## Features
 
**Pomodoro Timer:**
Automatically alternates between 25-minute focus sessions and 5-minute breaks. Includes start, pause, and reset controls.
 
**To-Do List:**
Add tasks, check them off as you go, and create new ones by pressing Enter. Completed items are visually struck through.
 
**Notes:**
A freeform text area for capturing ideas, references, or anything that comes up during a session.
 
**Time-Based Themes:**
The interface detects the current time of day and applies one of three themes: nNight, morning, or afternoon, adjusting the colors of all elements automatically.
